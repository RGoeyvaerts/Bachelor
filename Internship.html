Project information

  • Category: Security, All round
  • Client: VanRoey
  • Project date: 22 May, 2022

Summary

We did our intership with VanRoey, VanRoey is managed service provider that offers a wide range of services for various businesses and organizations. They started using SentinelOne almost a year ago and tasked us with comparing SentinelOne with Microsoft Defender to prove which one is better based on certain criteria. This internship dives into how these two XDR solutions stack up.

We'll go beyond just traditional Endpoint Detection and Response (EDR) features, and also analyze their full Extended Detection and Response (XDR) capabilities. We started by maing our plan of action and then we started doing microsoft learns to get a better understanding of Defender and Intune which wil be using to simulate the actual deployment that VanRoey uses. After a week or so we received the licenses and rights to both XDR solutions and we started our research.
We will research the following topics:

  • SKU's & Features
  • Management
  • Deployment
  • Testing
  • S1 RangerAD
  • Defender for Identity
  • Integrations
After we finished our project and documented everything we had to present our findings we did this twice once for the support team and once for the security and Microsoft team. After this we spent the final month working allongside the support team to get a better understanding of how they work and what they do.
